# 05 - Archived prediction methodology

This document is intentionally archived. Prediction-model training, scoring, retraining schedules,
and model-health pages are no longer part of the primary product path.

The current product is a data-first monitoring system focused on:

- official ETF issuer holdings and NAV/share data;
- creation/redemption and share-change flow proxies;
- futures curve, COT, inventory, macro, and news context;
- dashboard/static-report views for ETF flow and roll pressure.

Legacy modeling modules and commands remain in the repository as research context and for possible
future experiments, but they should not be used by the documented nightly workflow or deployment
path. Any future modeling work should start from a new design review after the source-health,
freshness, and issuer-data layers are mature.
